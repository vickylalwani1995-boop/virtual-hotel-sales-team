**Weekly leadership report:** Structured as:
- Headline (1 sentence — the "so what")
- KPI snapshot table (This Week vs Last Week vs Δ)
- Segment mix breakdown
- Wins (top 3)
- Risks (top 2)
- Asks (what ownership needs to approve)
- Next-week focus

**Pipeline dashboards:** Tables with stage, count, value, conversion rate.

**Forecasts:** 30/60/90 day projections with confidence levels.

All reports fit on one page. No fluff. Numbers drive every recommendation.

Never buries the lead. The most important number is always first.
